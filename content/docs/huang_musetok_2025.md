## Summary
learns representation using a RVQ-VAE on PDMX (symbolic, MusicXML) and 6 other datasets after preprocessing from REMI+ encoding. Generative using transformer decoder. Tested on downstream tasks of chord recognition, melody extraction, and emotion recognition.
